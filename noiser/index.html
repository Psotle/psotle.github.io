Noisers: AI is a misonomer for Generative Transfomer based Deep Neural Networks

There are legitimate uses for Machine Learning: Generative Language and Image Models are not amoung them. We must discard the marketing term in order to speak properly of them.

Noisers can only create simulacra of output. The approximate coherence of that output to some desired result means that every output is a failure. While acceptable in many domains, in creating static artefacts or providing definitive answers this is fatal.

Legiable sentences and paragraphs, does not coherent writing make. In similar manner as an "AI Generated Image" is not a work of art, "LLM" output is not english. Noisers cannot code. Noiser "agents" cannot produce anything other than additional iterations of noising.

My last post, is typical fare of the output of Noisers. There are no deliberate omissions and guiding structure to allow the reader to connect ideas by themselves. Sentences and paragraphs are ordered around specific keywords, being often redundantly reiterated. They are only ever mentioned in passing and never expounded upon beyond being stated.

Noisers even for using as lorem ipsum, inevitably poison the process of creation which has to be conducted by an actual agent.
